Privacy Policy

Learn how AgriMentor collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how AgriMentor collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use our platform. By using AgriMentor, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We are committed to handling your data responsibly and transparently, with a focus on supporting sustainable and secure agricultural practices.

2. Information We Collect

The types of information we may collect include:

  • Account information such as your name, email address, role (farmer or expert), and profile details.
  • Usage information such as pages viewed, actions taken, and timestamps of activity within the platform.
  • Uploaded content such as images, notes, and other agricultural data you choose to submit for analysis.
  • Technical information such as IP address, device type, and browser information, where applicable.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the AgriMentor platform and its features.
  • To generate AI-based insights, predictions, and recommendations relevant to your agricultural needs.
  • To improve the accuracy, usability, and performance of our services.
  • To communicate with you about updates, support, security alerts, and important notices.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms & Conditions.

4. Data Protection and Storage

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. These measures may include access controls, encryption where appropriate, and regular security reviews.

Your data is stored on secure servers and retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data only in limited circumstances, such as:

  • With trusted service providers who assist us in operating the platform, subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • When required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of AgriMentor, our users, or the public.

Any third parties with whom we share data are expected to handle it in a manner consistent with this policy.

6. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, which can include:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you.
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • The right to request deletion of your data, subject to legal or contractual obligations.
  • The right to object to or restrict certain types of processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests.

7. Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, you can contact us at:
